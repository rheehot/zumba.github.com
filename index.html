The technology stack at Zumba Fitness®, like many companies' stacks, runs on open source technology. We gain many benefits by utilizing the community's efforts in making safe, reliable, and free open-source software.

We've decided that we want to give back to the community.

Open Source

Zumba Fitness® is proud to start the process of open sourcing some of the tools that our technology team uses on a day-to-day basis in the hopes that we can contribute something back to the community.

Blog

Sometimes concepts are just as important as code. As our team develops and maintains our international and high-traffic website, we encounter problems that our team has to solve. The goal of this blog is to share some of that experience with others, and hopefully have some of you share some experience with us.
